Caerwent Community Council

Educational Awards 2016


Each year the Community Council supports an Educational Award to two local schools, Rogiet Primary and Shirenewton Primary.


The award has been running for two years, challenges the junior sections of the two schools to create art (years 3&4) or literary (years 5&6) on a given subject.


This years topic was “Where I live” and as you will see some interpreted this as local whilst other slightly further afield.


The standard at both school was very high and the winners efforts are displayed below. Vouchers or book tokens paid for by the Community Council were given as prizes to the winners. The schools decided the prize structure to be awarded.


The Community Council would like to thank the two schools and in particular the Head Teachers for allowing their pupils to take part.
